Guida Rapida alla Fine del Mondo

(A Brief Guide on the End of the World)

It is the title of a book curated by a Sardinian climate activist for the notoroius Friday for Future youth movement, and it deals with the contemporary challenges and threats that rapid, uncontrolled climate change poses on us.

The book is meant to spark a dialogue among people from all walks of life on the subject, debunk myths and clarify misconceptions, illustrate compelling science-based data, and give hope, but not without a sense of urgency.

The aforementioned urgency feel was the primary factor behind the creation of this flyer; the idea that the world is catching fire and temperatures are rising above critical levels. I wanted to capture this idea in a sort of hollywood-esque illustration, to immediately catch the viewer's attention and convey a sense of imminent natural catastrophe, a.k.a. the "end of the world".

The intent was to stand out from most designs which are, at times, very simplistic and with a heavy focus on the "green" aspect of the climate revolution.
