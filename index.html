Who is Andrea?

I'm a designer and web developer with a passion for aesthetics and a commitment to delivering quality work.

As a graphic designer, I understand color theory, typography and composition, and this enables me to craft visually appealing graphics that effectively communicate messages and have a strong identity. Whether it's designing logos, brochures, or digital assets, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project.

As a developer, I strive to apply the same concepts of aesthetics and functionality. Rather than building a website that just "works", I go through a meticulous process to first make sure to adhere to UI/UX principles, and then to write robust and clear code.
